How It Works
Live in Minutes, Not Months

Three steps from photo to full drug information — no pharmacy degree required.

1
Patient Uploads a Photo

The patient takes a photo of their medicine bottle or uploads a doctor's prescription directly from their phone or web browser.

2
AI Identifies & Fetches FDA Data

Claude AI identifies the medicine name, dosage, and manufacturer. We then cross-reference the FDA drug label database for verified safety data.

3
Patient Sees Clear, Safe Information

The patient receives plain-English side effects, warnings, condition-specific flags, generic alternatives, and drug interaction alerts — all in seconds.

Architecture Designed for Healthcare

Every layer of Pill Pal is built with HIPAA-aligned principles. We make compliance easy for your team.

🔒
HIPAA-Aligned Architecture
Technical and administrative safeguards aligned with the HIPAA Privacy and Security Rules. BAA available on request.
View HIPAA page →
🏛️
FDA Drug Database Powered
Medicine information sourced from OpenFDA — the official US government drug label database. Authoritative, government-verified data.
🔐
TLS Encrypted Transmission
All data transmitted between client and server uses TLS 1.2 or higher. No plaintext connections permitted in production.
🧠
Images Never Stored
Medicine images are processed entirely in server memory and never written to disk, object storage, or any persistent system.

BAA Available on Request

Revocable API Keys
