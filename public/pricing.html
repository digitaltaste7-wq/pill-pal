FAQ
Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Can I change plans later?
Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle with no penalties.
Is there a free trial?
We don't offer a free tier, but you can email us at sales@pillpal.app to discuss your needs before committing to a plan.
Do you offer a BAA?
Yes, a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is included with the Enterprise plan to support your HIPAA compliance obligations.
How long does integration take?
Less than one day. Our REST API is well-documented and most teams are live in a few hours. See our API Docs.
What drug database do you use?
We use the FDA-verified OpenFDA database for US medicines, with an AI fallback for Indian and international medicines not yet in the FDA dataset.
What if I exceed my scan limit?
We'll notify you as you approach your limit. You can upgrade instantly or purchase additional scans. We won't cut you off without warning.
